About 2M

At 2M, our mission is simple yet powerful — to connect those with surplus food to those in need. We work with individuals, restaurants, and communities to reduce food waste and ensure every meal makes a difference.

Every contribution matters. Whether you're donating a family meal or helping us spread awareness, you're becoming a part of a movement built on compassion, sustainability, and change.
